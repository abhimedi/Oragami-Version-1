Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. Camel's have humps
  2. Camels can attack by spitting
  3. Camels are a way of transportation

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that can change their scales color
  • Changing skin color is important to camouflage
  • They have a really long tounge and have two eyebals that can in different directions

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are types of birds
  2. Pigeons migrate in the winter
  3. Pigeons can move their wings ten times per second and maintain heartbeats at the rate of 600 times per minute, during the period of 16 hours.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  1. Pandas usually eat bamboo
  2. Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown)
  3. Though pandas are a species of bear, they never attack humans unless provoked

Interesting Facts about Cicada

  • Cicadas usually live for 15 to 21
  • Cicadas are really big compared to normal insects
